The Bottom Line

CBR's 44 pool villas on Chaweng Beach are naturally positioned for the two highest-value segments: couples/honeymoon (beach + private pool + romance) and family/multi-gen (multiple villas, beach, activities). The wellness rebrand amplifies both — 88% of wellness tourism is "secondary" (people seeking wellness as part of a broader trip). Digital nomads and long-stay travelers stabilize low season. The star reclassification from 3→4 remains the single highest-leverage action to unlock visibility across all segments.
