Key Takeaway for CBR

Every segment is growing, but at very different rates. Digital nomad services (21.3% CAGR) and solo travel (14.3% CAGR) are accelerating fastest. Bleisure (12.7%) offers the longest length of stay. Honeymoon (6.8%) is slower-growing but high-value per booking. Family travel is the volume king — 47% of all trips. The strategic play for CBR is to layer these segments: couples/honeymoon as premium core in high season, families for volume, and digital nomads + bleisure for low-season occupancy stabilization. The global trend toward fewer, longer trips and experience-led booking directly benefits a beachfront pool villa resort with wellness positioning.
